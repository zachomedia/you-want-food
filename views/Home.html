You Want Food

You Want Food is an application which displays information about the University of Waterloo Food Services. In addition to a listing of locations, You Want Food also provides the weekly menu and Region of Waterloo Public Health inspection results.

You can also subscribe to have You Want Food email you the menu daily.

New Features

Recently added to You Want Food are:

  • Region of Waterloo Public Health inspection results
  • Tabbed design on the Outlet page

Data Sources

Data is used under licence from:

  • University of Waterloo (through the Open Data API)
  • Region of Waterloo (through the Open Data Catalogue)
