You Want Food

About You Want Food

You Want Food is an application which displays University of Waterloo Food Services outlets and menu.

In addition to this web application, you can also subscribe to have You Want Food email you the menu daily.

Information Source

Information is provided by the University of Waterloo Open Data API. You Want Food requests updated information every 30 minutes.
